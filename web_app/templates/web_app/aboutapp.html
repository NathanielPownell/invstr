About invstr.



After becoming interested in stock investing and trading in early 2019, I have been using various platforms to invest in stocks. many of these platforms lack interface simplicity. When someone has just begun learning about stocks I believe a simple, bare-bones design works best.

Invstr. uses the idea of "Paper Trading" which is using a fake account balance and "pretend" money to invest and trade in stocks. Some platforms online offer this functionality but I believe it was an afterthought rather than the focus of these platforms.

My mission is to provide a simple interface where people can learn about stocks completely free, without risking any of their hard-earned money. I believe if I had access to a tool like this when I had just began my investing journey, it could have saved me lots of money.
